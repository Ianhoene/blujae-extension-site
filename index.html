Chrome Extension

Understand job fit faster.

Blujae helps users compare job listings against their experience, skills, preferences, and goals so they can quickly understand how well a role aligns.

Privacy Policy Terms of Use

What it does

Blujae analyzes job listings and combines page details with a saved user profile to generate role-fit insights.

What users can add

Users can provide skills, work preferences, compensation goals, certifications, education, career history, and optionally a resume during onboarding.

How it works

The extension stores profile information locally in Chrome and sends analysis data to backend services when users request job analysis or resume parsing.

Product Overview

Key capabilities

Job page scanning

Blujae reads visible job information from supported pages so it can analyze role requirements and present fit insights to the user.

Profile-based matching

Saved profile details such as skills, goals, and work preferences are used to make analysis more relevant to each user.

Resume-assisted setup

Users may upload a resume during onboarding to help prefill profile fields and improve job-matching context.
